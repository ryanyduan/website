IEEE Ottawa Robotics Competition

About the Ottawa Robotics Competition

The annual IEEE Ottawa Robotics Competition encourages students from Grades 5 to 12 to develop an early interest in science, technology, and engineering.

In teams, students will design and build an autonomous robot using LEGO® MINDSTORM kits to compete in a full day event filled with exciting challenges. In addition, teams will be judged on a written report and a poster presentation documenting their designs.

Win a Robot Arm! ORC Holds Robotics Arm Contest

As part of the IEEE Ottawa Robotics Competition’s (ORC) 15th anniversary, we will be giving away ten robotic arms so teams can join the ORC and compete in our latest Green Arm Challenge. The objective of the Green Arm Challenge is to place bins of recyclable materials from a loading dock onto the appropriate conveyor, quickly and accurately, by using a robotic arm.
